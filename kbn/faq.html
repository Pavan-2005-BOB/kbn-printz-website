Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept for custom designs?

We accept high-quality JPG, PNG, and SVG files. For best results with custom shapes, we recommend using a PNG file with a transparent background.

How long does it take to process an order?

Standard orders are typically processed and shipped within 3-5 business days. Custom orders may take an additional 2 business days.

Do you ship across India?

Yes! We ship to all locations within India. Shipping times may vary depending on your location.

What is your return policy?

Since our products are custom-made, we cannot accept returns unless the item arrives damaged or there is a mistake on our part. If you have any issues with your order, please contact us immediately so we can make it right.
